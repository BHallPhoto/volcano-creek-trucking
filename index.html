About Us

1Servicing & Maintaining

At Volcano Creek, we specialize in professional trucking services with a commitment to responsible forest stewardship. Serving both public and private landowners, our team combines industry expertise with modern equipment to safely and efficiently harvest timber while preserving the long-term health of forest ecosystems.

Our services include selective logging, road building, slash management, reforestation planning, and ongoing forest maintenance. We work closely with forestry consultants and regulatory agencies to ensure all operations meet environmental standards and support sustainable land use goals.

Whether it's managing wildfire risk, promoting new growth, or supporting habitat diversity, Volcano Creek is dedicated to maintaining the balance between resource use and environmental care.

2Reliability

At the core of our operations is an unwavering commitment to reliability. We understand the critical importance of consistency and timeliness in the trucking and logging industry. Our experienced team, well-maintained fleet, and streamlined logistics ensure that every project is completed safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

Whether it's hauling timber or supporting large-scale forestry operations, our clients trust us to deliver dependable service every time—because when it comes to your business, delays aren't an option. We pride ourselves on building long-term partnerships rooted in performance and trust. No matter the challenge, we’re prepared to rise to the occasion and keep your operations moving forward without interruption.

3Log Hauling & Low Bed Services

Our trucking division specializes in reliable and efficient log hauling and low bed transportation services, tailored to meet the demands of the forestry and construction industries. With a fleet of well-maintained, heavy-duty trucks and experienced drivers, we ensure the safe and timely delivery of timber from remote job sites to mills or processing facilities. Our team is well-versed in navigating challenging terrain and adhering to strict safety and environmental standards.

In addition to log hauling, we offer low bed services for transporting heavy equipment such as excavators, bulldozers, and other machinery. Whether it's moving equipment between worksites or delivering new units, our low bed solutions provide the flexibility and capacity required for oversized loads. We take pride in offering dependable service that keeps your operations running smoothly and on schedule.

California Air Resources Board (CARB)

Clean Air Act Compliance

At Volcano Creek Trucking, we’re committed to clean air, environmental responsibility, and regulatory compliance. We operate in full accordance with the California Air Resources Board (CARB) Clean Air Act standards and work proactively to reduce our environmental impact.

Clean Truck Check / CARB Compliance Testing

Our fleet undergoes regular Clean Truck Check (CTC) inspections, as required by CARB. These inspections ensure that all vehicles meet California’s emissions standards for heavy-duty diesel trucks. Our in-house testing protocols and maintenance schedules are aligned with the state's clean air goals, ensuring that every vehicle we operate is compliant and emissions-efficient.

Certified CARB Compliance Mechanic On Staff

We employ a CARB-certified compliance mechanic who oversees testing, emissions repairs, and documentation. Having a certified professional on-site allows us to act quickly and effectively to maintain our fleet’s compliance with CARB's HD I/M Program Requirements. This ensures that Volcano Creek Trucking stays ahead of regulatory changes and operates clean, reliable equipment every day.
